Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with a BSc in Computer Science (Second Class Upper) from Olabisi Onabanjo University. What sets me apart from most early-career candidates is simple: I don't just build models, I ship them.

My production-deployed Early Warning Food Price Monitor serves real users across Northeast Nigeria, forecasting commodity prices using SARIMA, XGBoost, and Facebook Prophet. That's the kind of end-to-end ownership I bring to every problem.

I work across the full ML stack: data engineering, feature engineering, model training, evaluation, and deployment. I'm currently deepening my expertise through the AWS AI & ML Scholars Program, DataCamp's ML Engineering track, and WorldQuant's Applied Data Science Lab.

I believe the best ML work happens at the intersection of rigorous science and real-world constraints. Production teaches you what research papers don't.
